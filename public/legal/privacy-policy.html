Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Advocate A. Ashvathaman Allimuthu ("we", "us", or "our") operates the official portfolio website. We are committed to protecting the privacy of visitors, constituents, and users who access this platform.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you fill out contact forms, newsletter subscription fields, or civic feedback boxes. This may include:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number / WhatsApp Contact Details
  • Content of messages or feedback submitted to our office

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is strictly used to facilitate democratic engagement, address local constituency issues, and respond to direct inquiries. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Respond to legal help requests, civic complaints, and feedback.
  • Send updates regarding regional campaigns, court orders, or community assembly programs.
  • Verify public concerns before raising them in state committees or judicial filings.

3. Data Protection and Security

We implement appropriate technical security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. We do not sell, trade, rent, or transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Your data remains strictly confidential within our official secretariat and legal offices.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external news reports, government portals (such as MyGov), and social media platforms. These third-party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. We hold no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites.

5. Cookies Policy

We use minor technical cookies or local storage settings to enhance browser load speeds and save navigation preferences. No demographic profiles or marketing trackers are used on this site.
